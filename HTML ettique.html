ETIQUETTE OF NATIONAL ART GALLERY

  • Register before you visit the gallery

  • Please walk carefully while you are at the gallery

  • Kindly distance yourself and please do not touch the artwork.

  • Please turn off the camera flash as the high light intensity may damage the artwork.

  • Activate silent mode on your phone while in the gallery.

  • Supervise your children while in the gallery.

  • Be considerate to people with disabilities, seniors, and pregnant women.

  • Food or drinks are not allowed in the gallery.

  • Store prohibited items in the provided lockers.

  • Speak at a low volume to respect other visitors.

  • Our gallery assistants are ready to help you.
