Gallery REKA

About Gallery REKA

Gallery Description:
Located on the 1st floor, this gallery provides facilities for the display of photography, textiles, fashion, prototypes, models, posters, architecture, and instruments. The gallery features a 36.52-meter wall, with a ceiling height ranging from 3.6 to 4.2 meters, and two mobile side panels measuring 2.44 meters x 1.22 meters. This gallery also features Ultra Violet Filter.
